About us

College admissions can sometimes be an entire course in and of itself. UniForMe is dedicated to making the college admissions process more efficient for all. After users select colleges that they are interested in, UniForMe graphically displays information about the colleges on a number of criteria, making it easy for users to compare them against many factors and find the one for them.

This project was initially made by Abu A, Abdullah Z, Rayyan M, and Julian B from the Bronx High School of Science as a submission for the 2023 Atomhacks hackathon. Further changes were made by Abu.
